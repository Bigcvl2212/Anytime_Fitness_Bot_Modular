How to Get Your Square Access Token
⏱️ This takes 2-3 minutes! Follow these simple steps.
Step 1: Open Square Developer Portal
  1. Click here: Square Developer Dashboard
  2. Log in with your normal Square email/password
Step 2: Create App
  1. Click "+ Create App"
  2. Name it "Gym Bot" (or anything)
  3. Click "Save"
Step 3: Get Access Token
  1. Click "Credentials" in left menu
  2. Find "Production" section
  3. Click "Show" next to "Access Token"
  4. Copy the token (starts with "EAA...")
  5. Paste it in the form above
Step 4: Get Location ID
  1. Same page, find "Location ID"
  2. Copy it (looks like "L1234ABCD5678")
  3. Paste it in the form above
✅ Done! Now complete the registration form.
